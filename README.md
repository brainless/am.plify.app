# Amplify (am.plify.app)

A place for founders and business owners to help each other. Help with sharing a Product/Service on a Reddit post, a Twitter thread, an Intagram post or any other social platform. Engage with intent and agency.

Note: In this network we may use bots and AI based tools to find content but the engagement has to be genuinely our own. We can even take help from AI in composing a response but the entire process (from findind to posting) cannot be outsourced to AI based tools - you will be warned and/or banned.

## How Does It Work

- Founders or business owners sign up to am.plify.app - `Founder/Business Owner Profile`
- Share your Product/Service - `Particulars Of Product/Service`
- Find something you want to help out with - `Browse Products/Services`
- Important: try out the Product/Service or know about it from the founder - `Know the Product/Service`
- Help out by engaging about the Product/Service on social media, local club, etc. - `Help The Product/Service`
- Points for helping out - `What Do I Get?`

## Founder/Business Owner Profile

- **Name**: Fill your first and last name
- **Bio**: Add your basic bio
- **Social Links**: Add links to all your social media accounts
- **Activity Feed**: We will list how you have helped out others

## Particulars Of Product/Service

- **Product Name**: Name of Product/Service
- **Links**: Website, Google Maps location, social media accounts
- **Business Domains**: Select 5 relevant tags
- **Problem Solved**: What problem does this solve?
- **Target Audience**: Who is your audience?
- **Audience Location**: Where is your audience?
- **Help Needed**: Select what kind(s) of help you need to amplify your Product/Service
- **Trial Access**: Select how you want helpers to try out your Product/Service before they help you

## Browse Products/Services

- **Homepage Browse**: See all Products/Services listed on am.plify.app
- **Smart Filters**: Use filters for online/offline/geography/domain, etc.
- **Auto-Save**: Filters get automatically saved when you apply
- **Discovery**: Find Products/Services that you are interested in

## Know the Product/Service

- **Online Software**: Try it out yourself
- **Paid Software**: Register and share your account email/username and we will notify the owner
- **Local Services**: You should ideally be in the vicinity to provide valuable feedback
- **Genuine Knowledge**: You must have minimum knowledge of what you're helping with - no blind promotion
- **Global Products**: Online products from anywhere in the world are fine to help with if you can genuinely try them

## Help The Product/Service

- **Local Services**: Leave genuine reviews on Google Maps, become an actual customer if needed
- **Online Products**: Share on Reddit posts, engage on Twitter threads, write reviews
- **Social Media**: Engage authentically on relevant platforms where the audience exists
- **Submit Proof**: Take screenshots of your help and submit through the product's form on am.plify.app
- **Daily Limits**: Strict limits on how much help you can provide per day to ensure quality

## What Do I Get?

- **Points System**: Earn points immediately upon submitting proof of help
- **Automatic Rewards**: Points are given automatically unless the original owner disputes
- **Community Recognition**: Your help activity is displayed on your profile feed
- **Network Building**: Connect with other founders and business owners in the community
